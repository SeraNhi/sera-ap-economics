Monopoly

Understanding single-firm market dominance.

Monopoly Market Structure

A monopoly exists when one firm controls the entire market for a product with no close substitutes.

Characteristics

  • Single Seller
  • High Barriers to Entry
  • Price Maker
  • Long-Run Economic Profits

Exam Focus

Understand profit-maximization where MR = MC and analyze deadweight loss on monopoly graphs.
